Ceratosaurus "Horned Lizard"
"sir-At-a-sawr-us"

Length: 8m
Height: 2.2m
Area: North America and Europe (Portugal)
Climate: Tropical
Period: Jurassic
Family: Ceratosauridae

Ceratosaurus was a carnivorous dinosaur,
likely feasting upon small herbivores,
such as Dryosaurus.

Known for its nasal horn and forehead
crests, Ceratosaurus is well known
as a predator of the Jurassic period.
It is also one of the few dinosaurs
to have fossils from its different
stages of life.
