Confuciusornis "Confucius Bird"
"Kon-foo-see-orn-iss"

Length: 0.5m
Height: 0.2m
Area: Asia (China)
Climate: Humid
Period: Early Cretaceous
Family: Confuciusornithidae

Confuciusornis was a small bird
that is known for it's 2 long
tail-feathers. It is also one of
the few prehistoric animals that
scientists know the color of.

Confuciusornis is know to be 
piscivorous, as it's diet 
included small fish. It is a
common prey item for medium 
sized carnivores.
