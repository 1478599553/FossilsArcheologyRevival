Allosaurus "Other Lizard"
"Al-owe-sore-uss"

Length: 8.5m
Height: 3m
Area: North America and Europe
Climate: Dry
Period: Late Jurassic

An iconic predator of the Jurassic, notable 
for its distinct eyebrow ridges and long 
arms featuring large claws. A larger variety 
of sizes has resulted in many different
 species of Allosaurus being named with
 Allosaurus fragillis being the 
 most well documented.
